Stremio Addon Setup

Install the Telegram Stremio addon to access your media through Stremio.

1

Copy the Addon URL

Copy the manifest URL below to your clipboard.

{{ request.base_url }}stremio/manifest.json
2

Open Stremio

Launch Stremio on your device.

3

Go to Addons

Navigate to the Addons section from the main menu.

4

Install the addon

Paste the URL and confirm install.

Tip: Stremio must reach this server address.

Additional Information

  • Network: Server must be reachable from your Stremio device.
  • Content: Movies and TV shows from your configured database are supported.
  • Sync: Library updates are reflected automatically.
